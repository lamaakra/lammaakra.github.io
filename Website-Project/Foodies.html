About Us

We've all been through the struggle of having a lot of ingredients in the house but not knowing what to cook, or wanting to cook something new but barely have anything from the list of ingredients in the recipe we looked up online. Foodies allows you to find new recipes on the spot, without having to go outside to buy the ingredients that you don't have. Saving you the time you would have spent searching for the ingredients and the hassle of getting the right ones. Foodies allows you to cook from the comfort of your home using only the ingredients that are available to you. Saving you time, money, and stress since 2018.

Create, Cook, Enjoy.
